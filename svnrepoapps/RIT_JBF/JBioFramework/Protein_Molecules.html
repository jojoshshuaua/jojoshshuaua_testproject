Protein Molecules

This page contains links which may be used to view the three dimensional structure of protein molecules using the Chime Netscape plug-in. Chime may be downloaded from http://www.mdli.com/chemscape/chime/download.html.

While viewing a molecule using the Chime plug-in, you can rotate the molecule by clicking and dragging with the left mouse button. You can change the way that Chime displays the molecule by selecting commands from a pop-up menu. Click the right mouse button to bring up the pop-up menu.

For more information about Chime, see http://www.mdli.com/chemscape/chime/chime.html.
