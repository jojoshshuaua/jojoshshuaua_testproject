Separation conditions

 

Before running the separation it is important to specify the conditions (pH range and % acrylamide).

 

The  drop down menu will allow you to select the pH range you want to view.

 

Additionally, you can specify a range by selecting "Enter A Range" and typing in a min and max pH:

                       

There is also "% Acrylamide" drop down menu to allow you to select which to use.
